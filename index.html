ABOUT US

Welcome to Pavansut Minerals, a distinguished name in the realm of minerals and solutions, established in 2017 by a team of seasoned experts with decades of collective experience dating back to the golden era of the ceramic industry in India. Our journey began with a vision to revolutionize the sector, and today, we stand as a beacon of excellence.

Pavansut Minerals boasts a rich legacy, with our partners and skilled workforce immersed in the nuances of various industries since the inception of the ceramic boom. With a diverse multi-mineral portfolio, we proudly serve an array of sectors including paint, ceramic, rubber, adhesive and sealant, paper, fibreglass, concrete, rubber, plastic, and more.

Committed to innovation and quality, our solutions are tailored to meet the dynamic needs of modern industries. At Pavansut Minerals, we blend expertise with a passion for excellence, ensuring that our clients receive unparalleled products that empower their growth and success.
